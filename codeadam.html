Bring Code Adam to Your Workplace

There is no cost to participate in Code Adam. Simply fill out the form below. The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children® will mail you a free Code Adam Kit which includes:

  • An employee training video.
  • A break room poster explaining the program.
  • Two decals to place near entrances and exits announcing your participation in the Code Adam program. If you need additional decals, please include the quantity in the comments section below.

Code Adam materials are currently undergoing changes and will be mailed as soon as they are complete. Thank you for your patience!
